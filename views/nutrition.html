Cystic Fibrosis

Cystic fibrosis (CF) has long been associated with malnutrition. However, due to early diagnosis, nutritional supplements, and increased prevalence of obesity in the general population, overweight, and obesity in the CF patient population is becoming a concern. Nutrition Controller address the adverse outcomes of overweight and obesity by advising on balancing diet.
